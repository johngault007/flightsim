## Operational Context

### Dispatch Summary
Lost Bay Aviation completed its inaugural premium leisure charter from Pensacola to Marathon in the Florida Keys.

The mission established the company’s initial operating profile as a boutique regional charter operator focused on coastal routing, premium leisure transport, and realistic Florida aviation operations.

### Operational Continuity
This was the first official Lost Bay Aviation career mission.

The flight originated from the home hub at KPNS and repositioned the aircraft into the Florida Keys, creating logical follow-on opportunities for South Florida, Bahamas, and island-adjacent charter work.
