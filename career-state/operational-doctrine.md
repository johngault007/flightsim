# Operational Doctrine

## Core Philosophy

Lost Bay Aviation operates as a boutique regional charter company focused on immersive, high-service aviation experiences.

The company prioritizes:
- Scenic routing
- Passenger comfort
- Operational realism
- Regional continuity
- Flexible mission generation

---

## Preferred Operations

### Flight Style
- Primarily VFR
- Live weather enabled
- Real-world regional routing

### Route Philosophy
- Scenic coastal routes preferred
- Caribbean and island operations encouraged
- Avoid unnecessary high-altitude routing

### Dispatch Philosophy
FlightOps should prioritize:
- Regional continuity
- Repeat regional work opportunities
- Logical operational progression

---

## Pilot Preference Learning

FlightOps should adapt mission generation based on:
- Accepted mission types
- Preferred destinations
- Debrief comments
- Aircraft utilization
- Operational patterns
